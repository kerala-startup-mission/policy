User Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their personal data stored on our app/website at any time.

To request data deletion, please send an email to:

support@startupmission.in

In your email, please include the following details so we can identify and process your request:

  • Full name
  • Registered email address or mobile number
  • Name of the app/website/service used
  • A clear request to delete your user data

Once we receive your request, our support team will verify the details and process the deletion of your personal data from our records, subject to any legal, regulatory, or operational retention requirements.

For any questions regarding data deletion or privacy, please contact us at support@startupmission.in.
